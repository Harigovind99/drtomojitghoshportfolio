ASSISTANT PROFESSOR · ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE & MACHINE LEARNING

Tomojit Ghosh

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the Wright State University, Dayton, Ohio. I am interested in developing algorithms for visualization, feature selection, and classification using the underlying geometric structure of data. Broadly my research interest lies in the theory and application of machine learning, artificial intelligence, optimization, mathematical modeling, and the application of pattern recognition and machine learning in Omics (transcriptomic, genomics, proteomics, and metabolomics) data. Currently, I'm also exploring generative models, zero-shot learning, etc.

If you are interested in collaborating with me, please email me at tomojit.ghosh@wright.edu.  ·  Google Scholar ↗
